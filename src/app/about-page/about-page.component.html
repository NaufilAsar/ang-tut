About Us

We are a small group of students from Government Polytechnic Mumbai who sought to overcome a problem as our Final Year Major Project which we faced in Online Shopping. We were given a task in the Course 'Computer Networks' to create a theoretical lab with Networking Setup for a budget of 1Lac for 10 PCs and their networking peripherals with them being of highest quality and as well as not over-budget. We found the task very easy but at the same time tedious to do so since different products were at different prices at different sites and of different qualities. Today majority of people prefer to buy-in products quickly from E-commerce giants such as Amazon and Flipkart who fluctuate prices for profit. This website was designed to provide customers with information about the products they wish to buy and also allowing them to find the best price for them across multiple sites. All free of cost.
